About ELIDEK

The Hellenic Foundation for Research and Innovation (ELIDEK) is a key funder of academic research in Greece, supporting innovation and scientific excellence across multiple disciplines.

Funding Range

Projects receive funding from €100,000 to €1,000,000, supporting research across 1-4 year durations.

Organization Types

Collaborating with Universities, Research Centers, and Enterprises to drive innovation forward.

Rigorous Evaluation

Each project undergoes thorough evaluation by independent researchers to ensure quality and impact.

Our Mission

ELIDEK plays a crucial role in the Greek research ecosystem by:

  • Funding high-quality research projects across all scientific disciplines
  • Supporting researchers at various career stages
  • Promoting interdisciplinary collaboration
  • Ensuring transparent and fair evaluation processes
  • Facilitating knowledge transfer and innovation
